# Privacy Policy Template

## Information We Collect
- Account information (name, email)
- Report submissions and associated images
- Location data (with explicit consent)
- Usage analytics and performance data

## How We Use Information
- Process and categorize civic reports
- Improve AI analysis capabilities
- Send notifications about report status
- Generate community impact statistics

## Data Protection
- All uploaded images are automatically processed to blur faces
- Location data is anonymized for public display
- Personal information is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits and updates

## Your Rights
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction or deletion
- Opt-out of non-essential data collection
- Download your data in portable format
